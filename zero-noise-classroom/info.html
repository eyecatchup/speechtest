Zero Noise Classroom v0.1.6

by Manuel Llorens

The free noise level display and stopwatch for the cooperative classroom.

It helps students be aware of the noise level in the classroom during cooperative learning activities, and moderate it accordingly.

When the time is over, the percentage of time that the noise level has exceeded the optimal level is displayed, so the teacher can use that value in gamification techniques.

Adjust the microphone sensibility in your operative system for best results.

Code partially adapted from https://github.com/cwilso/volume-meter by Cris Wilson.

Zero Noise Classroom uses fonts from Google Fonts, IcoMoon scalable icons, metronome sound from http://metronomer.com and alarm sound by Mike Koening.

Special thanks to Julio Lozano for testing.
